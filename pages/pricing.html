Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do you offer a refund policy?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with your Premium or Premium Plus subscription.

How are ingredient scans counted?

Each time you upload a photo and analyze ingredients, it counts as one scan. For free users, these reset at the beginning of each month.

Is there a family or group plan available?

We're currently developing family plans. For now, each subscription is for a single user. Contact us for business or group inquiries.
