Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't Recibee completely free?

AI-powered ingredients recognition and recipe generation cost quite a bit of money to run. To grow Recibee sustainably without compromising our service quality, we need to cover our costs through premium subscriptions.

What are free and premium uses?

Free uses allow basic ingredient recognition with limited scans per month. Premium uses give you more scans, enhanced recipe suggestions sorted by calories and health ratings, plus access to our upcoming mobile app's premium features.

What is Recibee's mobile app?

Our upcoming mobile app will feature native ingredient recognition and an expanded recipe library. Premium subscribers will automatically get access to all premium app features when it launches.

How do the plan limits work?

If you go over your scan limit, we'll nicely ask you to upgrade. Free users get 30 scans per month, while premium users enjoy up to 500 scans per month.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Where can I ask more questions?

If you have any questions about our plans or features, please contact our support team. We're always happy to help you get the most out of Recibee.
